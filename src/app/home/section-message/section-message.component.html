PRATIK DHIRENDRASINH GARASIYA
Principal, D.N. High School (Upper Primary)

A land full of greenery with modern facilities, a land of discipline and cleanliness, a land of action of revolutionaries, and natural environment is famous all over the world, spreading knowledge and creating the best citizens. It is an honor for me to have the opportunity to serve in a school that has been in constant operation since 1916 till date.

A School that inculcates culture and education with modern methods in accordance with today's time, means D. N. High School Upper Primary School. A son of D. N. has great a treasure of strength, virtue, courage and leadership.

Along with Education, I have extreme desire to do something special by focusing on things like various sports activities, exercise and yoga for the health of my children. Along with all my teachers and other staff members, I have cultivated a family spirit and remained loyal to the institution and school. I am serving and will continue to serve the administrative work. I have deep feeling in my heart that a student of my school will go to any corner of the world will become proficient in any field. I am confident that my student will swim differently in the field of education. I strongly believe in Work but not in Luck.
